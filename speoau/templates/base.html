{% block vision %}

Vision

SPEOAU Student Chapter aims at providing each affiliate with opportunities for personal and professional development through an amalgamation of events round the year including Distinguished Lectures, Workshops, Soft Skill Development Seminars and a plethora of contests. We strive to provide each and every student member a common platform to showcase their acumen in front of eminent industry veterans and compete with peers. The learning programs are aimed at making student members well acquainted with Reservoir, Production and Drilling Engineering and also get familiar with Health, Safety and Environmental factors of Oil and Gas industry. It also focuses on unconventional energy sources.
